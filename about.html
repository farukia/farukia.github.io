I'm Ayesha.

While writing about myself in the third person would be fun, I don't think it'd be ideal. So, therefore, let's get to the basics.

I'm a 14-year-old girl who has an interest in too many things to count. As an avid author, I pour my soul out into words, specifically tales of fantasy and sci-fi (or, in the occasional instance, realistic fiction in the form of short stories). My taste of writing genre mirrors that of what I read, and my bookworm mind is often submerged somewhere far from I am, in the depths of Hogwarts or Camp Half-Blood. I'm also interested in computer programming, especially with games and websites. With this and writing combined, I developed this website of my own to display them. I also blog, and I try to get something out every week I have the time.
